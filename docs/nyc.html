New York

This is an image of New York City that I grabbed from a royalty free site and did some interesting edits to. What I was going for in this image was a more comic book type of look for the image and then the two bars across the top and the bottom to resemble the old 3D glasses that had the red and blue films on them. This was just a way for me to play around inside of photoshop and explore some of the tools they offer and broaden my skillset when it comes to utilizing photoshop as a photo editing tool.
